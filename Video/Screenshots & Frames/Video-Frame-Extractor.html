Step-by-Step Tutorial

Step 1: Upload Video

Click on the upload area or drag & drop your video file to begin the extraction process. The tool supports all major video formats.

Step 2: Preview Video

Once uploaded, you'll see the video preview on the left and a real-time frame display on the right. The current time (in milliseconds) is shown below the video.

Step 3: Set Extraction Options

Choose between Batch Frame Extraction (to extract multiple frames at specified intervals) or Single Frame extraction (to capture specific moments). Select your preferred output format (PNG, JPG, or WebP).

Step 4: Extract Frames

Use the "Extract Frames" button to start the extraction process. For single frames, you can navigate through the video using the playback controls and extract frames at any moment.

Step 5: Download Frames

After extraction, your frames will be displayed in the gallery. You can download individual frames or all frames at once using the provided download options.
