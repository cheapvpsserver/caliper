Step-by-Step Tutorial

Step 1: Paste Your Text

Copy and paste your article or text into the left text area.

The analyzer will automatically calculate basic stats like total words, characters, and reading time.

Step 2: Configure Options

Customize your analysis with these options:

  • Tokenization: Choose to ignore case and filter stop words
  • Keywords: Set phrase length (1-3 words) and how many top keywords to show
  • Sort: Sort results by frequency or alphabetical order
Step 3: View Results

The right panel will display your keyword density results, including:

  • Keyword phrases found in your text
  • Frequency count for each keyword
  • Density percentage for each keyword

Use the "Copy Table" or " Download CSV" buttons to export your results.

Tips for Best Results
  • Use the "Ignore case" option for more accurate results
  • Filter stop words to focus on meaningful keywords
  • Try different phrase lengths to find long-tail keywords
  • Analyze competitors' content to identify their keyword strategy
