Step-by-Step Tutorial

Step 1: Input Your Text

Enter your text in one of two ways:

  • Type or Paste Text: Directly type or paste your text into the text area
  • Upload File: Click the "Choose File" button to upload a TXT, PDF, or DOCX file from your device

The tool processes your text in real-time as you type or make changes.

Step 2: View Instant Statistics

Your text will be analyzed immediately, displaying comprehensive statistics:

  • Basic Counts: Characters, non-blank characters, words, spaces, sentences, and lines
  • Advanced Metrics: Not empty lines, estimated pages, reading time, speaking time, and reading level

Click the "Copy Text" button to copy all statistics to your clipboard at once.

Step 3: Analyze Word Frequency

View detailed word frequency analysis on the right panel:

  • Word Rankings: See the most frequently used words in your text
  • Occurrences: Check how many times each word appears
  • Percentage: See the percentage of total words each word represents

Customize your view:

  • Use the "Show Top" input to adjust how many words to display
  • Click "Export CSV" to download the word frequency data as a CSV file
Tips for Best Results
  • For accurate reading level calculation, ensure your text has at least 10 words and 2 sentences
  • Use the file upload feature for quick analysis of entire documents
  • Export CSV data for further analysis in spreadsheet applications
  • Copy statistics to easily share them or include in reports
  • Remember that very long texts (over 10,000 characters) may slow down performance
