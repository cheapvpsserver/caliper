Step-by-Step Tutorial

Step 1: Input Your Text

Paste your long text into the left text area. The tool will automatically count the words for you.

For best results, input at least 50 words to get meaningful summaries.

Step 2: Configure Your Summary Options

Customize how your text is shortened:

  • Format: Choose between Paragraph or Bullet Points format
  • Length: Select Short, Medium, Long, or Detailed summary length
  • Keywords: Review and select the most important keywords to focus on

You can also drag and drop keywords to customize their order and importance.

Step 3: Generate and Use Your Summary

Click the "Shorten & Summarize" button to generate your summary. Your results will appear in the right output area.

You can:

  • Copy the summarized text with one click using the "Copy text" button
  • Download the summary as a TXT file
  • Adjust the options and regenerate the summary if needed
Tips for Best Results
  • For academic texts, use the "Detailed" length option
  • For social media posts, use "Short" length with bullet points
  • Always review the summary to ensure it captures the key points
  • Experiment with different keyword selections to get different summaries
