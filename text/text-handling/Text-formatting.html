Step-by-Step Tutorial

Step 1: Input Your Text

Paste or type your text into the left text area. The tool will automatically display comprehensive statistics about your text, including character count, word count, and reading time.

You can use the Undo and Redo buttons to revert or restore changes as needed.

Step 2: Choose Formatting Options

Select from a wide range of formatting options in the right panel:

  • Case Formatting: Convert text to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, and more
  • Space Management: Add, remove, or adjust spaces, tabs, and line breaks
  • Text Manipulation: Reverse text, sort lines, remove punctuation, and more
  • Character Filtering: Remove unwanted characters, special characters, or duplicates
  • HTML & Code: Format HTML tags, escape special characters, and more
  • Encoding & Decoding: Decode HTML entities and URL-encoded characters

Click "All" buttons in each category to apply all options in that category at once.

Step 3: Get Your Results

Your formatted text will appear in the left text area instantly. You can:

  • Copy the formatted text with one click using the "Copy" button
  • Clear all text to start over with the "Clear" button
  • Use the Find & Replace feature to make targeted changes
  • Review the updated text statistics
Tips for Best Results
  • Start with a clear understanding of the formatting you need
  • Apply one formatting option at a time to see its effect
  • Use the Undo button if you don't like the result of a formatting action
  • Combine multiple formatting options for more complex text transformations
  • Check the text statistics to ensure your formatting meets your requirements
