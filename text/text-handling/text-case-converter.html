Step-by-Step Tutorial

Step 1: Input Your Text

Enter or paste your text into the left text area. The tool will automatically display statistics about your text, including character count, word count, and line count.

You can use the Undo and Redo buttons to revert or restore changes as needed.

Step 2: Choose Case Format

Select from a wide range of case formatting options:

  • Sentence Case: Capitalizes the first letter of sentences
  • Capitalized Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of major words
  • lowercase: Converts all text to lowercase
  • UPPERCASE: Converts all text to uppercase
  • snake_case: Converts to lowercase with underscores between words
  • dot.case: Converts to lowercase with dots between words
  • Slugify Text: Converts text for use in URLs

Additional options include Random Case, Reverse text, and various space management tools.

Step 3: Get Your Results

Your converted text will appear in the right output area instantly. You can:

  • Copy the converted text with one click using the "Copy" button
  • Clear all text to start over with the "Clear" button
  • Try different case formats to find the best fit for your needs
Tips for Best Results
  • Use Sentence Case for regular text and paragraphs
  • Use Title Case for headlines and titles
  • Use snake_case or dot.case for code variables and file names
  • Use Slugify for creating SEO-friendly URLs
  • Experiment with different formats to find the one that works best for your specific use case
